✧˖°.Class letter✧˖°.

We just wanted to take a moment to say something real, to all of us. Thank you for being the chaotic, hilarious, supportive, and unforgettable group that we've shared this journey with. Every single one of us has brought something special to this class, whether it was the loud laughs, the weird jokes, the group panic before exams, or the last-minute "who has the homework?" moments. We've been through a lot together. the good, the bad, and the absolutely unexplainable, and somehow, we made it work (kind of). At the same time, we want to apologize to each other for the times we weren’t at our best. For when we argued over group projects, didn’t include everyone, or maybe said things without thinking. For not always being patient, for losing our cool, and for not showing up for each other the way we should have. We’re human. We messed up sometimes. But the important part is that we grew from it, and we stuck together anyway. Even when things got messy, this class always found a way to come back and laugh, support each other, and keep going. That says a lot. And honestly, we wouldn’t trade this wild, unpredictable, hilarious group for anything. So here’s to us for surviving the assignments, the exams, the last-minute projects, the inside jokes, and the memories we’ll never forget. Thank you for being part of something we’ll always remember.
